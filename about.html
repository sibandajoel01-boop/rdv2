About RelocationForDubai

An independent relocation resource built for UK residents — not for estate agents, not for visa brokers, and not for anyone trying to sell you Dubai.

Why This Site Exists

Moving to Dubai from the UK involves navigating a tangle of information — much of it incomplete, outdated, or written by someone trying to sell you a property or a company formation package.

This site exists to fill that gap. It is written from the perspective of someone who has been through the process — understanding what HMRC actually requires, how the UAE visa system works in practice, and what living costs look like once you're settled and not relying on a company relocation package.

The goal is simple: give you enough accurate, honest information to make a genuinely informed decision about whether Dubai is right for you — and if it is, to help you do it properly.

What We Cover

  • UAE Visas & Residency — all routes for UK nationals
  • UK & UAE Tax — the SRT, HMRC obligations, and the UAE tax environment
  • Work & Business Setup — employed, freelancer, and company formation routes
  • Cost of Living & Property — real numbers, not averages from 2019

Affiliate Transparency

Some pages on this site include affiliate links to third-party services — visa application platforms, business formation companies, insurance providers, and similar. These are clearly marked. Where we include a recommendation, we have either used the service ourselves or evaluated it based on community feedback and public reputation.

We do not accept payment for editorial placement. Affiliate relationships do not influence the content of our guides.
