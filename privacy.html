Privacy Policy

How RelocationForDubai.com collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: January 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how RelocationForDubai.com ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information obtained through your use of this website (relocationfordubai.com). By using this site, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our contact form, we collect the information you voluntarily submit, including your name, email address, and the content of your message. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we may collect standard technical information when you visit, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited. This is used for site analytics and security purposes only.

Cookies

This site may use cookies to improve your experience. These may include analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics) and functional cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Where required by law, we will request your consent before placing non-essential cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and messages
  • Analyse website traffic and improve content
  • Maintain the security and performance of this website
  • Comply with any legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Affiliate Links & Third-Party Services

This website contains affiliate links to third-party services. When you click an affiliate link, you will be directed to a third-party website governed by that site's own privacy policy. We have no control over third-party data practices and encourage you to review their policies before providing personal information.

We use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use this site. These services may set their own cookies. For more information on Google Analytics data practices, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry, and are then deleted. Analytics data is retained in accordance with the settings of the relevant analytics platform.

5. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

If you are located in the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR):

  • The right to access personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to processing of your data
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the contact page.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information submitted through this site. However, no internet transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of data transmitted to or from this website.

7. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please use our contact form.
